About Me

My name is Zeeshan and I am currently studying Applied Computer Science at Thomas More Campus Geel. I have completed my bachelor degree in electrical engineering from National University of Science and Technology (NUST) in Pakistan. Due to my interest in programming, I decided to pursue a career in the field of computer science and AI.

I have worked as a front end web developer for more than one year in a software company. I find great joy in organizing community events as it teaches project management, negotiation, team coordination and initiative. In my free time, I like to learn new technologies and work on personal projects.

After completing my graduation, my primary goal is to work as a software developer with a reputed and innovative tech company such as Google or Microsoft. I aim to gain hands-on experience, deepen my technical expertise, and understand large-scale software development practices. Over the years, as I grow professionally and sharpen my skills, I aspire to launch my own software company—creating impactful solutions and contributing to the tech ecosystem as an entrepreneur.

TravelBook

Web Development Project

Personal Project
Live Demo

Introducing TravelBook: A Social Media Platform for Travelers! ✈️ I worked on individual TALL stack project and made an exciting web application called TravelBook, a platform that brings travel enthusiasts together to share their journeys, discover new destinations, and connect with like-minded explorers.
✍️ Share Travel Experiences: Users can create posts with descriptions and photos to showcase their adventures.
🌟 Interactive Feeds: Like, comment, and engage with posts.
🔔 Notifications: Stay updated with follow requests, likes, and comments.
🗺️ Search and Filters: Discover users and posts by location or interests.
🌤️ Weather Integration: Get 5-day weather forecasts for your favorite destinations.
🌙 Dark Mode: Browse comfortably with a dark mode feature.

Key Learnings:
  • Handle complex database relationships
  • Implement real-time features using Livewire
  • Modular Development
  • Enhance UI/UX with responsive design and accessibility
